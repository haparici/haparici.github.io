I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at the MIT Computational Psycholinguistics Lab. I am interested in theoretical and experimental semantics/pragmatics.

My most recent work focuses on the interaction of context and meaning. I am particularly interested in the different types of context-sensitivity involved in the processing of relative vs. absolute adjectives.
